Our Services

CDR

SkillAssessmentAustralia provides top-notch CDR report writing services to help you prevent possible denial from Engineers Australia. This comprehensive service includes 3 Career Episodes, One Summary Statement, and one Continuing Professional Development. Every client receives a unique CDR Report based on their requirements.

TRA

Upgrade your career and take the next step to reach Australia through all Trade Recognition Australia (TRA) Programs! Get in touch with us for eligibility requirements and application information for Australian citizens, as well as immigrants.

ACS RPL

Through our Writing Experts, get approval on your Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) report for assessing individual abilities for those who would like to move to Australia and are looking forward to starting a job in the ICT sector but don't have any ICT credentials or educational ICT qualifications.

KA02 Knowledge Assessment

Our experts can help you migrate to New Zealand with a successful Knowledge Assessment (KA02) report. We will make sure that your evaluation process is successful and that you can demonstrate your expertise and experience in the field of your choice.

CDR Report Review:

We provide editing and proofreading services for CDRs to help clients who want to review their CDRs with experienced professionals before they submit them to Engineers Australia. We will ensure that you have provided information about the engineering skills and knowledge that meet Australian standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

The requirements for skill assessment in Australia depend on the particular visa type you are applying for. Generally, most visa applications require you to provide evidence of your qualifications and employment experience, as well as undertake a skills assessment conducted by an approved assessing authority. The assessing authority will assess your skills and determine whether they meet the required standard for your chosen occupation. You may need to provide evidence of your English language proficiency, have your skills and qualifications verified by a Recognised Qualifying Authority (QRA), and provide additional documentation to prove that you satisfy the other requirements for the visa.

The cost of skill assessments depends on the occupation and assessment body. Generally, the cost ranges from AUD $400 to $2,000.
